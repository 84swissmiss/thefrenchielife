**French Bulldogs are not regular dogs.** I say that with love. They're wonderful, hilarious, stubborn little creatures ‚Äî but they have very specific needs:

- **Brachycephalic airways** ‚Äî They can't breathe well in heat, with tight collars, or during heavy exercise
- **Sensitive skin** ‚Äî Allergies, hot spots, and fold infections are basically a Frenchie rite of passage
- **Joint issues** ‚Äî Their compact, muscular build puts stress on joints, especially as they age
- **Pulling power** ‚Äî Don't let the small size fool you. George pulls like a sled dog when he spots a pigeon
- **Temperature sensitivity** ‚Äî They overheat in summer and freeze in winter. High maintenance? Maybe. Worth it? Absolutely.
